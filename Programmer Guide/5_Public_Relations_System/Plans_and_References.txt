PR SUBSYSTEM - DESIGN PLANS & REFERENCES
====================================================================

Design Language: Material Design 3 (M3 / Material You)
Primary Palette: Indigo (Primary), Violet (Secondary), Error (Red)

Accessibility Standards (Older Demographic):
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- Touch Targets: Min 48px height for all buttons.
- Form Width: 1100px max-width for cards to reduce visual clutter.
- Hierarchy: Numbered sections with helpful supporting text.
- Typography: Large, readable font-sizes (e.g., 1.1rem for inputs).

Component References:
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- PRRoom.jsx: Main orchestration component.
- PublicRelations.css: Source of truth for M3 form card styles.
- PRAdminWorkspace.jsx: Template for officer-only admin forms.

Future Integration (Module 1):
